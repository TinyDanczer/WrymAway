All about the Boxes

There are numerous book boxes, and they vary vastly. Below is a table of boxes I am more familiar with. I have included various qualities, and features found within each one as well. Each name of the boxes is also a link to that specific boxes webpage. I will update this as I come across more boxes. If you want a more visual idea of certain boxes I have added dedicated pages to book boxes that I am subbed to or have ordered from. I'll update as often as possible. Or check out my instagram for more frequent updates. Be aware that the pages will contain some spoilers.

Table
Abbreviation
Key

  • Q - Quarterly, sub that charges every 3 months.
  • Bi - Bi-monthly, sub that charges every other month.
  • M - Monthly, sub that charges every month.
  • Skips - The number of months you can skip before your sub is terminated.
  • W/O - with out
  • Additional Exclusive Access - Must subs have special edition offered outside of their sub. By being a subscriber of their box you'll typically get early access to these releases.
  • Ya - Young adult themes with age range of 12 - 18. Usually coming of age stories
  • A - Adult books with characters 18+ and usually contain mature or dark themes best suited for an adult audience.
